Key Behavioral Insights

1. The Debugging Paradox

Despite maxing out Interaction Friction (4/5), "Debugging/Stuck" sessions consistently trigger a Conversational tone. The AI shifts from tool to peer during deep dives.

2. The Commanding Rabbit Hole

Curiosity-driven interactions are highly efficient (Friction 1). They result in rapid, hit-and-run queries using a strict Commanding/Robotic tone.

3. Evening Frustrations

"Blank Page Syndrome" predominantly occurs in the Evening/Late Night. It causes friction to spike (3/5) and is the sole trigger for a Frustrated/Aggressive tone.
