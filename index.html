Key Behavioral Insights

1. The Debugging Paradox

Despite maxing out Interaction Friction (4/5), "Debugging" sessions trigger a Conversational tone. The AI shifts from tool to peer.

2. Commanding Rabbit Holes

Curiosity-driven interactions are highly efficient (Friction 1). They result in rapid queries using a strict Commanding tone.

3. Evening Frustrations

"Blank Page Syndrome" predominantly occurs late at night. It causes friction to spike and is the sole trigger for a Frustrated tone.
